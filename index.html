PISP Acoustic Synthesis Demonstrations

360° Listener Rotation

Demonstrates spatial audio rendering for a listener moving around a fixed sound source. Shows dynamic channel balance and environmental reverberation effects.

Rotating Sound Source

Illustrates binaural audio synthesis for a fixed listener with a 360° rotating source. Features Doppler effects and HRTF-based spatialization.

Project implementation of "Novel View Acoustic Synthesis with Physically Informed Sounding Particles" (ICCV 2025)
